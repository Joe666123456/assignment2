World demographic and economy report



World demographic overview

The top two countries with largest population in the world: China and India, with 37% of the world's population. Africa is the continent with second largest population in the world, with about one billion people, which is nearly 15% of the population in the world. There are 733 million people in the Europe, accounting for 11% of the world's population.

Latin America has about 600 million people, accounting for 9% of the world's population. North America, dominated by the United States and Canada, has 352 million people, accounting for 5% of the world's population. Oceania has the smallest population, with only about 35 million people, accounting for 0.5% of the world's population. The main group is Australia.


Demographic attritude analysis



With the improvement of economy in many developing countries, these countries have low natural growth rate in population. This is mainly reflected in low level of birth rate, followed by relatively high level of the death rate compared to the previous years. In some developed regions, such as Italy and Russia, the natural growth rate is negative, which means the population start to decrease.

Introduction on typical countries




Germany

German industry specialises in developing and producing complex products, especially capital goods and innovative production technologies. Compared with other national economies, Germany's industrial ratio is much larger. In total, 7.5 million people are employed in industry and processing.




Australia

The argriculture take 24% in Australia economy, which is relatively high proportion. Australia is one of the world's important producers and exporters of agricultural products. There are 135,997 farms for farming and animal husbandry, which cover 61% of the land in Australia.


America

As a long-term world "hegemon", the United States has always maintained its leading position in economic size. The service plays an significant role in the economy, of United Stateswhich is 79% in 2017.


China

At the beginning of the establishing of China, it was a typical agricultural country. Since the reform and opening up in1978, its economic structure has gradually become more reasonable, and it has developed in the direction of optimization and upgrading.
